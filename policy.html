Your Rights & Our Commitments

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

Transparency

You have the right to know what data we collect, how we use it, and who we share it with. We provide clear information about our data practices.

Your Control

You can access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. You can also withdraw consent at any time.

Data Retention

We only keep your data as long as necessary for the purposes we collected it, with specific retention periods for different types of data.

Detailed Privacy Information

Types of Data We Collect

WADABA collects various types of personal data to provide our water data and citizen science services:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and organizational affiliation
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates and geographic information for water monitoring
  • Water Quality Data: Measurements, photos, and observations submitted by citizen scientists
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform and services
  • Professional Information: Background and expertise for training and collaboration purposes

How We Use Your Data

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide water data services and platform access
  • Research: For scientific research and environmental monitoring (with appropriate anonymization)
  • Communication: To send newsletters, updates, and respond to inquiries
  • Training: To organize workshops and capacity building programs
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze usage and improve our services

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: When you agree to participate in citizen science or receive communications
  • Legitimate Interest: For research and environmental protection purposes
  • Public Interest: Water resource management and environmental monitoring
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with environmental and research regulations

Data Retention Periods

We retain your data for specific periods based on the purpose:

  • Water Quality Data: Up to 10 years for long-term environmental monitoring
  • Contact Information: As long as you maintain an active relationship with WADABA
  • Training Records: 5 years for certification and follow-up purposes
  • Usage Analytics: 2 years for platform improvement

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations) - Delete data
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data in certain circumstances
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for voluntary data processing

Data Sharing and Transfers

We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • Research Partners: Anonymized data with academic institutions and research organizations
  • Government Agencies: Aggregated water data for policy development
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate our platform
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Data may be transferred internationally for research collaborations, with appropriate safeguards in place.
